YouTube

Video games have always been an important part of my life. I wouldn't be the person I am today or have the friends that I have if it wasn't for them. However, I'm beginning to reach the point in my life where I understand I cannot play games as often as I used too. I wanted a way to remember the time I have spent, so I decided to create a Youtube channel. This channel has existed for a long time now as I used to use it to post DJ mixes back in 2015.

So I started with my favourite game of all time. Destiny 2. I have sunk in over 1000 hours into this game and it was worth every minute. The timing was quite opportune as large portions of content from the first three years of the games lifetime are being "vaulted." So I set about recording the campaigns from the three years of available story. It took a little while to do, but the process of recording and producing gave me a few additional skills such as basic video editing and photoshopping for the thumbnails.

I was pleased with the overall result, and my dedication to upload daily. Now I have an established routine, I plan on uploading more gaming content over time. There are so many more video games that stand out from my teenage years that I want to memorialise. Anything that I upload in future will be sure to appear here as well.
