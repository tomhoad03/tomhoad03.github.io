Film Reviews

Like most people, I watched too many films over the Coronavirus lockdown. It was probably a waste of time, but I wanted something to show for it. So I decided to use twitter as a platform for reviewing films and sharing that with my followers. I didn't really care if anyone read them, but it was a good way of remembering what I saw over lockdown. There are so many films we get to see and then forget that would make for great recommendations.

None of the original reviews exist on my account anymore, so I'm keeping a record of all 90 or so here. I still review the odd film on twitter, but only the best ones that I know I will want to see again.
