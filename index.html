Formula 1 Prediction Website

The largest project I have ever attempted was an F1 Prediction Website. Part of my coursework for A-Level computer science, I had to create a website that made using the client-server model with both a front-end, back-end and using an API to communicate between the two. Giving detailed documentation, I had to go through stages of analysis, design, development and evaluation, similar to methodologies used by professional developers. My passion for Formula 1 and a new love for coding during this period motivated me to develop a website I am very proud of that was ultimately graded an A*.
